Jaypee Youth Parliament 2017

Biggest Youth Parliament of central India, Jaypee Youth Parliament 2017 a much-anticipated event is being organized on 22th & 23th April 2017 by the JUET Youth Concord under the aegis of JYC-Literary Wing.It is a simulated version of the Parliament of INDIA. It consists of The Lok- Sabha(lower house) and The Rajya-Sabha(upper house) witnessing participation of more than 800 students in the past Youth Parliaments.

Jaypee National Youth Parliament 2017

Jaypee Youth Concord is responsible for holding MUN & Youth Parliament.

The Youth Parliament holds three simulated councils of Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha whereas Model United Nations holds United Nations General Assembly. The main aim of organizing this event is to enable young people to be effective and expressive agents of change.The objective of the Youth Parliament is to teach youth democracy, equip them with debating skills, stimulate them to present their own thoughts and listen to those of others, how to follow through, advocate for an idea, and make it a reality.
